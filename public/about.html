What is SCALEFARM?
SCALEFARM is a new social action initiative project. It created in June 15, 2023. SCALEFARM targets crucial environmental problems that world faces such as huge amounts of household sustainable waste, use of soil with pesticides, and dehydrated land.
How we solve problems?
SCALEFARM has a unique approach to environmental issues. That is, saving environment without even $1 by using household materials. There are two ways: composting from household sustainable materials and vertical farming. You will find information in our Actions page.
Our founder writes
Hello everybody! I am Nikolaos Nikolov and I am 16 years old. I founded my first initiative project because I wanted to solve problems near me. We all hear in the news about climate change and its effects. We live it! But we cannot stay idle! We have to save our future! In one word: SCALEFARM.
We have a plan which is tested first of all in my home and it works. Composting our household sustainable materials is very simple and quick! While recycling average in EU is 27%, in Greece, where I live, it’s only 15%. Composting will happen easily, with a plastic bucket and your household waste. That’s it!
The soil that will be created has manure features. Now, vertical farming is needed. Think of a small vertical grocery in your balcony that will provide you high quantities of fresh fruits and vegetables. You will like it!
You can follow us! Stay tuned and if you are interested to join our team, send us an email! We are sure that we can save our future. We can save our environment. We just have to change habits in our homes!
NIKOLAOS NIKOLOV
